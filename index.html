☰ About ℹ

About Screen

About StudySpot UQ

StudySpot UQ helps students decide where to study on campus by showing live capacity, noise levels, amenities, and environmental conditions.

Key Features

  • 🔐 UQ Single Sign On and saved user preferences
  • ⭐ Favourite study spaces with availability alerts
  • 🗺️ Campus map with colour-coded study spaces
  • 📊 Live capacity and busyness ratings
  • 🔈 Noise level viewing and reporting
  • 🔌 Amenity filters and tag feedback
  • 🤫 Anonymous quiet reminders
  • ⌚ Smartwatch quick capacity view
  • 📳 Haptic notifications for useful updates
  • 📍 Location prompts when arriving on campus or at a study space

Interaction Style

The mock-up uses a touch-first mobile layout with haptic-style notification examples, matching a multimodal interaction approach.
